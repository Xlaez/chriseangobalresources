    • About Chrisean Global Resources

    Chrisean Global Resources was established in 2016 with the aim of giving African students the competitive space for foreign education. We have a wide range of courses for students in different departments and faculties. Some of the courses are Administration, Management, Engineering, Computer Sciences, Medicine, Pharmacy, General Practice Nursing, Multimedia and so much more. Chrisean Global Resources has its main office in Lithuania and some states in Nigeria. Using our educational application system, students can apply to any university in Europe. Our main goal is to make the process of searching, choosing and deciding on any university is simple, easy and quick in irrespective of the African country the student is residing. Please note, the application process and processing time is handled by each university. Visas are granted by the embassies, diplomatic missions and consular posts of the country of study.
